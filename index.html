Who We Are

CERES Global Enterprises is a Chennai-based agriculture support and consulting organization working closely with farmers, livestock owners, farmer groups, MSMEs, and institutions to improve farm incomes and strengthen market access. We design and implement practical agriculture projects that are financially viable, scalable, and sustainable, combining field-level knowledge, digital tools, and strong market connections.

CERES identifies suitable locations and develops collection and storage points where farm and livestock produce can be aggregated and moved efficiently to markets. Through careful assessment of local demand, production levels, and ground conditions, we help reduce transportation costs, prevent post-harvest losses, and ensure timely sales.

We support farmers and producer groups in finding buyers, converting enquiries into confirmed sales, and receiving payments directly. This ensures transparency, faster settlements, and better price realization. Our systems track quantities, prices, and payments clearly so producers always know the status of their sales.

CERES also assists entrepreneurs, farmer groups, and agribusinesses in accessing bank loans and institutional funding. We prepare Detailed Project Reports (DPRs) and the required technical and financial documents for banks, NABARD, and investors, helping projects secure timely approvals and funding.

To improve efficiency and accountability, we use simple automation, AI-based planning tools, and ERP systems to manage records, forecasts, inventory, and payments. These digital tools reduce errors, improve planning, and bring greater transparency across the value chain. Supply of fodder, straw, and seeds is provided where required as part of broader project support and market linkage activities.

By combining field-level understanding, digital systems, financial guidance, and market access, CERES Global Enterprises works as a reliable partner in strengthening agricultural livelihoods and supporting inclusive rural development.
